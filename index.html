UNI5, What is it ?

Enthusiastic people with the goal to create projects; ​ Incredible meetings with entrepreneurial experts in Montreal; ​ 1 day in each university, discovering awesome places in Montreal for teamwork; ​

Francophone or anglophone, tech or business, realizing the necessity to connect all students in the cosmopolitan city of Montreal, we decided to collaborate to make an event dedicated to the youth passionated by entrepreneurship. ​

UNI5, We Unify !

The second edition of UNI5 thus has an objective to give different students the opportunity to compete against each other while gathering and ultimately creating the first students-entrepreneurs network in Montreal.

Finally, just a lot of fun !

  • 60 students
